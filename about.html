Smart FM: Your All-in-One Facility Management Solution

Transform your facility operations with Smart FM, the comprehensive platform that empowers you to streamline processes, optimize costs, and achieve peak performance.

Why Choose Smart FM…

Comprehensive Facility Register

Manage every inch of your space with detailed data on buildings, floors, spots, geo-fencing, and interactive maps for clear visualization.

Streamlined Maintenance

Never miss a beat with PPM, PM scheduling, automated run-hour work orders, and efficient reactive maintenance management.

Reactive Maintenance & Service Requests

Manage reactive maintenance efficiently, handle ad- hoc requests seamlessly, and ensure timely resolution with the built-in helpdesk and automated work order creation.

Effortless Service Requests

Handle both ad-hoc requests and facility audits/inspections seamlessly, ensuring your team stays on top of every need.

Comprehensive Asset Management

Track and manage all your assets with ease, from comprehensive asset profiles to technical resource management with detailed tracking.

Contract & Inventory Management

Create and manage service contracts and subcontracts, optimize multi-store inventory, and monitor material consumption in real-time.

CService Level Agreements (SLAs)

Define clear response and resolution timeframes for seamless service delivery.

Enhanced Procurement & Budgeting

Simplify procurement processes, create and monitor budgets for contracts, services, and properties, and maintain tight control over costs. Intuitive Help Desk: Resolve issues promptly with an effective help desk featuring automated work order generation for BMS alarms.

Financial Control

Create and monitor accounting and finance for contracts, services, and properties, ensuring responsible and transparent financial management.

Workflow Automation

Streamline operations with configurable workflows, ensuring tasks are routed to the right people at the right time.

Data-Driven Insights & Reporting

Generate insightful reports, dashboards, and analytics to make informed decisions and track progress.

Secure Administration

Enjoy robust user management, role-based permissions, user license management, and detailed audit reports for complete control.
